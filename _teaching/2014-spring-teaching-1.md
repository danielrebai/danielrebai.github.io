Since Spring 2021, I've served as a teaching assistant for the Hydraulics course, supporting Civil Engineering students (2021) and Environmental Engineering students (2022-present). My primary focus has been leading exercises on fluid statics, one-dimensional modeling of pressurized pipe flows, and one-dimensional modeling of open channel flows. I have developed exam materials, assisted with grading, and coordinated hands-on experimental activities in class, including small flume experiments.

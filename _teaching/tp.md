Since Fall 2026, I have been serving as a Teaching Assistant for the Transport Processes course. I support students in understanding the fundamental principles governing momentum, heat, and mass transfer. My responsibilities include conducting tutorial sessions, assisting with problem-solving exercises, and providing guidance on transport phenomena applications in chemical and process engineering.

Topics covered in the course include:

* Conservation laws and transport equations
* Momentum transport and fluid flow
* Heat conduction and convection
* Mass diffusion and species transport
* Dimensional analysis and similarity
* Applications in chemical and biological systems
